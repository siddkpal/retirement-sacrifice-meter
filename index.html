What's actually being calculated

EMI

Standard annuity formula on your loan amount, rate, and tenure — the same maths your bank uses.

FV

Your EPF, NPS, and WeCare contributions are grown year-by-year as your salary rises, compounding at each instrument's own assumed return.

Opp.

The opportunity cost is the future value of investing your exact EMI amount in equity instead, at the same monthly cadence, for the same number of months.

Safe

The safe EMI ceiling reserves 15% of take-home for retirement, 40% for living costs, and 10% as a buffer — whatever's left is what you can safely commit to a loan.
